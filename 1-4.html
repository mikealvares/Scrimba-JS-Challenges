SCRIMBA - JSChallenge - Sum of All Primes


Sum of Prime Numbers up to and including the provided number.
A prime number is defined as a number greater then one and having only two divisors, 1 and itself. Eg 2 is prine number because it is divisable by one and two.
The proivided number may not be a prime.
eg:
sumAllPrime(10) = 17
sumAllPrime(977) = 73156
